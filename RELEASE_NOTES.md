## What's new in v0.2.0

- Day and Week views. Switch between Day, Week, and Month using the small labels next to the date up top. Day and Week show an hour-by-hour timeline that opens on your 8am–5pm window and scrolls to anything earlier or later.
- Week view highlights today's column so it's easy to see where you are in the week.
- All-day events get their own spot — a row across the top in Week view, and a side panel in Day view.
- Click a "+N" on a busy day in Month view to peek at everything scheduled that day.
- Cleaner, roomier event styling everywhere, and a tidier month grid that only shows the weeks that belong to the current month.

## What's new in v0.1.1

- Overlapping events now get a "Potential Conflict" flag. Dismiss it (with a 10-second undo if you misclick) or hit "Remind me later" to snooze it until it matters.
- Notifications before a meeting starts and when you have a conflict. Set the lead time in Settings → Notifications.
- Keep Todaymarks running quietly in the menu bar so reminders reach you, and start it automatically at login (Settings → Menu Bar).
- Events that have passed now dim and drop to the bottom of the day, marked "Done," so the list always shows what's still ahead.
- A "What's New" changelog now lives in Settings, so you can look back at what changed.
- Cleaner spacing, search, and Settings alignment throughout.

## What's new in v0.1.0

- First release. A small, native macOS calendar for your Apple Calendar.
- Month view with multi-day event bars and a resizable day agenda.
- Create, edit, and delete events on any writable calendar.
- Pick and recolor calendars, search, and a "How do I look?" camera mirror.
